Beginner playbook
A simple sequence that works
  1. Stop leaks: Remove or negate search terms spending with zero sales.
  2. Fix share gap: Shift budget toward campaigns where sales share > spend share.
  3. Control CPC: Lower bids where CPC is high and CVR is low.
  4. Scale winners: Increase budgets where ROAS is consistently strong.
  5. Investigate spikes: Large spend spikes without sales spikes = problem.
